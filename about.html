About SPPS Platform

Unlock academic excellence through predictive ML modeling, proactive warning alerts, and cohort tracking.

Aim of the Project

The **Student Performance Prediction System (SPPS)** is engineered to empower academic advisors and university administrators with proactive, data-driven forecasting utilities. By modeling predictive outputs from previous CGPAs, attendance levels, and core social factors, the platform determines potential risk groups with a verified statistical accuracy index of over **94%**. This early detection is key to deploying targeted support systems before term-end examinations.

Key System Pillars

Neural Inference Classifier

Evaluates complex grade arrays and study patterns immediately using stable local inference thresholds.

Proactive Risk Warnings

Flags cohort indicators when study rates, final term prep, or attendance rates slump under critical limits.

Institutional Audit Sheets

Download structured PDF or Excel transcripts for faculty interventions, ensuring complete security compliance.
