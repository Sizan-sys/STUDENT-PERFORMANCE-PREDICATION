How Predictions Work

Behind the elegant SaaS interface operates an institutional predictive system designed to evaluate and elevate academic performance safely.

1

Import Parameters

Load general student backgrounds, previous GPAs, attendance trackers, and study schedules safely into our central index system.

2

Feature Engineering

Weighted scoring models automatically process indicators like sleep hours, internet availability, and midterms to standardize performance signals.

3

Neural Evaluation

Our machine learning classifier computes the passing probability, evaluating standard errors to generate detailed confidence tiers.

4

Advisory Actions

System generates custom action steps (tutoring, check-ins, counselor alerts) depending on the registered student risk category.
